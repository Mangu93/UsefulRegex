Useful Regex.
Matching a typical username:
^[a-z0-9_-]{3,16}$ 

Matching a typical password:
^[a-z0-9_-]{6,18}$

Matching a hexadecimal number:
([a-f0-9]{6}|[a-f0-9]{3})

Matching an email:
^([a-z0-9_\.-]+)@([\da-z\.-]+)\.([a-z\.]{2,6})$

Matching an url:
^(https?:\/\/)?([\da-z\.-]+)\.([a-z\.]{2,6})([\/\w \.-]*)*\/?$

Matching an ip:
^(?:(?:25[0-5]|2[0-4][0-9]|[01]?[0-9][0-9]?)\.){3}(?:25[0-5]|2[0-4][0-9]|[01]?[0-9][0-9]?)$

Matching a html tag:
^<([a-z]+)([^<]+)*(?:>(.*)<\/\1>|\s+\/>)$
